About Me

Starting Jan 2016, I am Postdoctoral researcher at Microsoft Research - Inria Joint center, hosted by Dr. Laurent Massoulie, Dr. Mark Lelarge, and Dr. Milan Vojnovic.

Prior to this, I was Simons Postdoctoral Fellow at The University of Texas at Austin hosted by Prof. Francois Baccelli in fall of 2015.

I obtained my PhD at The University of Texas at Austin in August 2015 under supervision of Prof. Gustavo de Veciana.

I have also held research positions at Bell Labs New Jersey, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay, and Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore.

Research Interests

  • System optimization and algorithm design for scalable cloud computing systems

  • Applied queueing theory

  • Machine learning algorithms suitable for dynamic machine-human interactions
