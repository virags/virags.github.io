Biography

(Needs Work!!!)

Starting Jan 2016, I am Postdoctoral researcher at Microsoft Research - Inria Joint center at Palaiseau, France, hosted by Dr. Laurent Massoulie, Dr. Mark Lelarge, and Dr. Milan Vojnovic.

I obtained my PhD at The University of Texas at Austin in August 2015 under advice of Prof. Gustavo de Veciana. We developed algorithms and performance analysis for centralized content delivery infrastructure. In fall of 2015, I was Simons Postdoc Fellow at UT Austin hosted by Prof. Francois Baccelli. We provided new queueing models for analyzing performance in cloud computing systems. In summer of 2013, I was at Bell Labs, (now Nokia Bell Labs) where I worked with Dr. T. V. Lakshman and Dr. Murali Kodialam. At Bell Labs I was involved in developing protocols for content storage and delivery in data centers for low latency.

Before coming to the West, I worked at two top research institutes in India, namely, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay, and Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore. In 2010, I was Research Fellow at IIT Bombay. I worked with Prof. D. Manjunath and Prof. Bikash K. Dey and designed fast algorithms for in-network function computations. I obtained my Masters degree in Telecommunication from IISc, Bangalore, India in July 2009 under advise of Prof. Neelesh B. Mehta. We developed distributed algorithms for wireless cooperative relay selection.
